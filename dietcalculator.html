Fruit and Vegetable Consumption Calculator

What is the fruit and vegetable consumption calculator?

There is much confusion about what counts as part of the daily recommended fruit and vegetable consumption. The fruit and vegetable consumption calculator is here to help you calculate the amount of daily fruit and vegetables a person should eat to be healthy! All values are calculated from the CDC dietary recommendations on fruits and vegetables.


How does it work?

This page will calculate the amount of fruits and vegetables you eat daily! For this calculator, servings are counted as cups of raw fruits or vegetables, to follow CDC and other nutrient guidelines and their base units of serving sizes.
